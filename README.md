# Customer Behavior & Revenue Analytics Dashboard

## 📊 Project Overview

The **Customer Behavior & Revenue Analytics Dashboard** is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze customer purchasing behavior, revenue trends, customer satisfaction, and demographic insights. The dashboard helps businesses understand customer segments, identify top-performing product categories, and make data-driven decisions.

This project was built using **Power BI**, **DAX**, and **Excel Dataset** to transform raw customer data into actionable business insights.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze customer purchasing behavior.
* Identify top-performing product categories.
* Understand customer demographics and preferences.
* Measure customer satisfaction through review ratings.
* Evaluate subscription adoption among customers.
* Provide business insights through interactive visualizations.

## 🔍 Key Business Insights

* 📈 **Clothing** generates the highest revenue among all product categories.
* 👥 **Young Adults** contribute the largest share of total revenue.
* ⭐ The overall average customer rating is **3.75**.
* 🔔 Only **27%** of customers are subscribed.
* 💰 Average customer purchase value is **$59.76**.
